Quick summary

This premium 10-day tour combines Japan’s best driving experiences with cultural highlights. Start in Tokyo for exclusive showroom visits and evening car meets, cruise the bay in Yokohama for photo-perfect supercar shots, then head north to Fukushima for mountain-road drives on the Bandai–Azuma Skyline and drifting at Ebisu Circuit.

Why this trip is amazing

  • Private track day at Fuji Speedway with coaching
  • Visit top automotive attractions: Toyota Mega Web, Nissan Gallery
  • Supercar-chauffeured transfers and scenic coastal drives
  • Hands-on drift experience at Ebisu Circuit
  • Comfortable hotels + one traditional ryokan onsen night

Highlights from the itinerary

  1. Day 1 - Arrive Tokyo: welcome dinner and intro to JDM culture
  2. Days 2–3 - Tokyo: museum/showroom visits and Daikoku PA night meet
  3. Day 4 - Yokohama: Minato Mirai photoshoot & Nissan Gallery
  4. Day 5 - Fuji Speedway: private track session
  5. Days 6–7 - Hakone & mountain roads: onsen stay
  6. Days 8–9 - Fukushima: Bandai–Azuma Skyline and Ebisu Circuit
  7. Day 10 - Return to Tokyo and depart
